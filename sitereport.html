Development of theSite

Over the term, I have worked on developing my website, focusing on both the technical and design aspects of the user interface. For the design, I chose a clean and modern aesthetic with a minimalistic color scheme. Moreover,I have used Sky Blue as a main colour followed by purplish as a background colour and white as a text colour I used Google Fonts to choose a font that was easy to read and looked good on different devices. I also looked at other websites for inspiration and incorporated some of their design elements into my site.

On the technical side, I focused on creating a responsive site that would look good on different screen sizes. I used media queries and flexbox to ensure that the site was easy to navigate and user-friendly. I also made sure that the HTML and CSS code was well-organized and commented for ease of use and readability.

Overall, I am proud of the progress I have made on my website and am excited to continue improving my web development and design skills.
